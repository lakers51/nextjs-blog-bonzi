# 2-Day Vacation Plan in Shanghai
## Day 1: Visit the iconic Oriental Pearl Tower for stunning city views, then head to the Bund for a beautiful riverside walk along colonial-era buildings. After lunch, visit the picturesque Yu Garden for traditional Chinese architecture. Shop and have dinner at Xintiandi for a contemporary experience.
## Day 2: Start your day at the Jade Buddha Temple, then explore the modern art and cultural center of China, the Power Station of Art, located in a former power plant. In the afternoon, visit the Shanghai Museum for extensive collections of Chinese art and artifacts. End your day with a relaxing boat ride on the Huangpu River.
